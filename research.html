My research uses experiments and computational modeling to understand the laws that govern change in the variables relevant for language. Some of these variables are the spatial positions of speech articulators, the neural activation controlling production and comprehension, and the neural connectivity constituting linguistic knowledge. I am inspired by dynamical systems approaches to linguistics and cognitive science, like Articulatory Phonology and Dynamic Field Theory.
